# LeetCode Patterns

## Hash Map

Used when a problem requires fast lookup, counting, grouping, or checking whether a value has already appeared.

Common examples:
- Two Sum
- Group Anagrams
- Longest Consecutive Sequence

---

## Two Pointers

Used when working with sorted arrays, pairs, palindromes, or problems involving movement from both ends of a structure.

---

## Sliding Window

Used for substring or subarray problems where a window expands and contracts.

---

## Binary Search

Used when the input is sorted or when the answer can be searched over a range.

---

## Stack

Used for matching, parsing, monotonic patterns, or tracking previous values.

---

## Dynamic Programming

Used when a problem has overlapping subproblems and optimal substructure.

---

## Graph Traversal

Used for connected components, paths, grids, dependencies, and networks.

---

## Backtracking

Used for generating combinations, permutations, subsets, or exploring constrained search spaces.
